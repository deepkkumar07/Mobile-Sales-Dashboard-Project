📱 Mobile Sales Analytics Dashboard | Power BI
1. Business Problem

A mobile retail company operates across multiple cities and sells various smartphone brands through different payment channels. Management lacked a centralized reporting system to answer critical business questions such as:

Which mobile models generate the highest revenue?
Which cities contribute most to overall sales?
What payment methods are preferred by customers?
How do customer ratings impact sales performance?
Which customer age groups drive the most revenue?
How are sales trending over time?

Without a unified dashboard, decision-makers had to rely on multiple reports, making analysis time-consuming and inefficient.

2. Project Objective

To design and develop an interactive Power BI dashboard that provides a comprehensive view of mobile sales performance, customer behavior, and regional trends, enabling stakeholders to make data-driven business decisions.

Dashboard Goals
Monitor overall business performance through key KPIs.
Analyze sales trends across different dimensions.
Identify top-performing products and markets.
Understand customer purchasing patterns.
Evaluate payment method preferences.
Enable self-service analytics through interactive filtering.

Business Insights Derived
📌 Product Insights
Premium smartphone models contributed the highest revenue.
A small number of models generated a significant share of total sales.
📌 Geographic Insights
Metro cities showed stronger sales performance than smaller markets.
Regional analysis helps prioritize expansion opportunities.
📌 Customer Insights
Customers aged 25–45 contributed the majority of revenue.
Higher-rated customer experiences correlated with stronger sales performance.
📌 Payment Insights
Digital payment methods accounted for most transactions.
UPI emerged as a dominant payment channel.
📌 Operational Insights
Sales trends revealed specific high-demand periods.
Business can use these insights for inventory planning and promotional campaigns.
